I am an incoming PhD student at UT Austin, joining in Fall 2022, where I will be working with Neeraja Yadwadkar.

Currently, I am working as a Research Engineer at Microsoft Research India. My work lies at the intersection of Computer Vision and Accessibility with a focus on making all kinds of printed content accessible to people with print-impairment. I am a part of Technology and Empowerment group.

I am broadly interested in the area of Systems and Machine Learning with a motivation to build technology towards societal impact.

Before, I had been a Research Fellow and ML Engineer at Wadhwani AI, an independent, non-profit research instutite. I worked on developing AI-based systems for societal impact. Besides, I also developed an internal ML platform for orchestration and scheduling of large-scale ML jobs.

I graduated from IIT Guwahati in 2019 with a major in Electronics and Electrical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. During my undergraduate, I enjoyed my summers at Hanyang University, South Korea, and NVIDIA.
